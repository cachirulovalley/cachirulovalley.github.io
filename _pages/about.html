What is Cachirulo Valley?

A group of volunteers who promote the entrepreneur/tech ecosystem in Aragón (Spain), through free activities, workshops and talks.

Some of the things we have done recently:

  • Sept 2014: Participated in "Jacathon" [ES]
  • Sept 2014: Open Data with David Cabo [ES]
  • Feb 2014: SOSZ14 - This year's highlight!
  • Oct 2013: How to grow your startup's team
  • Sept 2013: Mobile products with Unboring y Frogtek
